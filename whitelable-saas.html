What is white label ERP software?

‘White label’ refers to a fully supported ERP software product or service that’s made by one company but sold by another. In essence, white-label solutions are products or services you can rebrand and resell as your own to your own clients. One way to assess whether your best suited to open source ERP software or a white label ERP solution is to take a look at the various pros and cons. Let’s start with open source ERP software.
